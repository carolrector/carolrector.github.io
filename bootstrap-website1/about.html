About Born to Review

Born to Review is a fan-made site dedicated to exploring Lana Del Rey’s albums and artistic eras. Rather than focusing only on the music itself, this site looks at each album as a complete experience, combining sound, visuals, themes, and cultural context. Lana’s work is known for its strong aesthetic identity, and each era she creates feels like its own world, which makes her discography especially interesting to analyze.

Over the course of her career, Lana Del Rey’s music has evolved significantly, moving from the cinematic sadness and glamour of Born to Die to the more introspective and personal storytelling found in her later projects. Each album reflects a different stage in her life and career, while still maintaining the emotional depth and poetic style that defines her as an artist. Because of this, her discography offers a unique opportunity to explore how themes like love, identity, fame, and nostalgia develop over time.

This site was created by a college student and long-time Lana Del Rey fan who has spent years listening to and connecting with her music. These albums have been part of different phases of my life, and my understanding of them has grown over time through repeated listens, personal reflection, and reading different interpretations online. Writing these reviews is a way to combine my interest in music, storytelling, and analysis while also sharing something meaningful with others.

Ultimately, Born to Review is meant to be both a personal project and a space for appreciation. Whether someone is a dedicated fan or just discovering Lana Del Rey for the first time, this site aims to highlight what makes her work so impactful and worth revisiting. My goal is to encourage others to listen more closely, think more deeply, and experience these albums in a new way.

All album artwork is used for educational and review purposes only.
