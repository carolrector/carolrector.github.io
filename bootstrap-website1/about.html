About Born to Review

Born to Review is a fan-made site exploring Lana Del Rey’s albums and artistic eras. Each album is reviewed not just for its music, but for its aesthetic, themes, and cultural impact.

Lana’s work evolves across eras—from cinematic tragedy to introspective storytelling— making her discography perfect for deeper analysis and review.
