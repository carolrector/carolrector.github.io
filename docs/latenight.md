## When You're ... Looking for a Late Night Bite

Late nights in Blacksburg almost always end with food, and luckily, there are a few reliable spots that stay open when everything else is closed. Whether you are heading home after a night out or just up late and hungry, these places are known for fast service, satisfying portions, and hours that go well past midnight. Expect classic comfort foods, easy grab-and-go options, and menus designed to hit the spot when you need it most. These are the go-to locations that keep the town fed long after the evening winds down.

### [Bennys ($)](https://www.bennysva.com/locations/marzanos/)

110 Draper Rd NW, Blacksburg, VA 24060 

* Blacksburg classic pizza spot with huge slices, and a constant line out the door. 
* It closes at 12 AM weekdays, and 2 AM on weekends. 
* Home of the first original Benny’s created by Hokie alums before they expanded down the east coast.

>Get an extra garlic or ranch dipping sauce, as well as hot honey on your slice!

### [DP Dough ($)](https://www.dpdough.com/)

215 N Main St, Blacksburg, VA 24060 

* A fast, classic calzone place in the heart of downtown Blacksburg that closes at 4 AM. 
* Best for a quick grab on your way home.

### [Cookout ($)](https://cookout.com/)

1311 S Main St, Blacksburg, VA 24060 

* In-store and drive through service for trays of fried food, burgers, milkshakes, and more. 
* Open through 3:30 AM most days and 4:30 AM on Fridays.
