Carol's Top 5 Songs

Guaranteed to levitate upon listening.

  1. Video Games by Lana Del Rey One of her most popular but insanely written songs.
  2. Let It Happen by Tame Impala Trippy but in the best way. Levitating.
  3. Two Ghosts by Harry Styles So much sentimental value for high school me.
  4. Friday I'm in Love by The Cure The first sunny day after a cold spell driving with the windows down. Life is worth living.
  5. Pool House by The Backseat Lovers So unique and passionately sung, tugs the heart strings.
